How it works.

From your closet to your keepsake, in four steps.

1

Reach out

Tell me about your fabrics and what you’re dreaming of. No idea is too small or too sentimental.

2

Send your textiles

Mail them or drop them off in Chattanooga. I’ll assess what the fabric can become and quote the work.

3

We design together

We settle the piece, the layout, and which parts of the fabric matter most to you.

4

Made by hand

I sew it one seam at a time and send it home with the scraps I didn’t use. Most pieces take 3–6 weeks.
